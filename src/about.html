About Us

Our Company Overview

Galaxy Worldwide Shipping, a distinguished member of Galaxy Group, has been a leading Auto Shipping company in the UAE since 2005. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted choice for fast, reliable, and high-quality shipping services. Whether you need to import or export brand new, used, or custom-made cars, boats, machinery, bikes, or trucks, Galaxy Worldwide Shipping facilitates seamless transactions to and from the UAE and the globe

Galaxy Worldwide Shipping provides comprehensive services for the delivery of Auto and machinery from USA, CANADA, Europe, Japan, South Korea and UAE to the rest of the world. we work without intermediaries, so we offer low prices for our customers. We closely follow the latest market trends and always try to offer the best way to our valued clients to deliver their Autos & Goods along the chosen route
